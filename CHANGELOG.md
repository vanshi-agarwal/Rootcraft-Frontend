## How to Update

When updates are released:

1. **Backup your project**: Always create a backup before updating
2. **Review the changelog**: Check what has changed
3. **Download the update**: Get the latest version from your purchase platform
4. **Manual merge**: Carefully merge changes if you've customized the template
5. **Test thoroughly**: Test all functionality after updating

**Note**: Updates may contain breaking changes. Always review the changelog before updating.

---

## Reporting Issues

Found a bug or have a feature request?

1. Check if it's already been reported
2. Contact us through your purchase platform
3. Provide detailed information:
   - What you expected to happen
   - What actually happened
   - Steps to reproduce
   - Browser and OS information
   - Screenshots if applicable
